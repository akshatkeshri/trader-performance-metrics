# Crypto Market Psychology: Sentiment-Driven Performance Analysis

An exploratory data analysis (EDA) investigating the relationship between retail trader performance on Hyperliquid and the Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index. This project uncovers clear behavioral biases and quantifies the financial impact of emotional trading across various market cycles.

## Key Insights & Alpha Uncovered
* **The FOMO Tax:** Over **72% of all trading volume** occurs during 'Greed' or 'Extreme Greed' phases, yet these phases yield the worst financial outcomes.
* **Extreme Greed Carnage:** Average losses balloon exponentially to **-$116.12 per trade** during Extreme Greed—over **8x worse** than neutral market conditions. 
* **The Reversal Trap:** Long positions (Buys) take the heaviest hit during market peaks (-$132.84 average PnL) due to aggressive top-buying right before sharp market liquidations.
* **Disciplined Neutrality:** Retail traders exhibit the highest win rates (~42.5%) and the tightest risk control when market sentiment is completely Neutral.
